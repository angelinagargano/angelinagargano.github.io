Healthcare Bill of Rights

This is a resource that gives patients a "Healthcare Bill of Rights" to bring to healthcare providers. This allows patients to prevent themselves from being untreated fairly and unlawfully.

GLMA Health Professionals Advancing LGBTQ Equality

GLMA is an organization that helps LGBTQ+ individuals find the appropriate healthcare providers that will meet their specific needs. It also elaborates on LGBTQ+ medical discrimination issues.

Cigna LGBTQ+ Health Disparities

Here, Cigna's website sheds light on the various ways people in the LGBTQ+ community are discriminated against in healthcare and the effects of this discrimination.

Groundswell Black Trans Fund

This fund strives to donate money to various black trans causes. Causes mentioned include housing, medical, media, religion, and more.
